Machine Learning Demand Forecasting Engine

An advanced interactive simulator demonstrating why traditional statistical models (Holt-Winters DES) fail in complex promotional markets, and how Machine Learning (XGBoost/Prophet) incorporates price elasticity and marketing spend to maximize forecast precision.

🔮 Real-Time Interactive Forecast Projection (18 Months)
📖 S&OP Insights: Understanding the Models

Traditional Statistical Model (Holt-Winters): Relies entirely on historical demand trends and historical seasonality. It is mathematically blind to real-world commercial activities (price changes, marketing pushes) occurring in the present, leading to a massive forecast error of 14.2%.

Machine Learning Model (XGBoost): Treat forecasting as a feature engineering problem. It incorporates current price elasticity indices, marketing promotional budgets, and calendar holiday indicators as features. As you adjust the sliders on the right, the ML model dynamically projects demand spikes and dips with an exceptional **7.4% MAPE** (Mean Absolute Percentage Error).

⚙️ Feature Engineering Inputs
Price Discount % 10%
Adjust the promotional discount level. A higher discount leverages volume price elasticity (demand increase).
Marketing Ad Spend (AED) AED 5,000
Adjust local advertising and social media budget. Generates media-driven demand lift.
Seasonal Context (Ramadan) Baseline
Simulate a high-impact regional seasonal surge. 0 = Off-Season, 1 = Baseline, 2 = Ramadan Peak.
