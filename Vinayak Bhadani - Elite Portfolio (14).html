About Me

I'm Vinayak Bhadani, a Supply Chain Analyst passionate about solving complex logistics puzzles through data science and advanced analytics. With over 3 years of professional experience spanning multiple industries, I've helped organizations achieve significant cost reductions and operational improvements.

My approach combines statistical rigor with business acumen, leveraging tools like Python, Power BI, and machine learning to transform raw data into actionable insights. I believe that every supply chain challenge is an opportunity to innovate and create measurable value.

Currently at ANDS in Dubai, I manage end-to-end supply chain operations, from demand forecasting and S&OP to 3PL coordination and customs workflows. Previously, I optimized inventory management at Global Automobiles and developed supply chain UI solutions at Fateinfo Technologies.

I hold a Master of Global Business with specialization in Global Logistics and Supply Chain Management from SP Jain School of Global Management, and a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Science & Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University.

Currently pursuing CPIM certification to deepen my expertise in production and inventory management. I'm also an active contributor to the supply chain community through published articles and knowledge sharing.
